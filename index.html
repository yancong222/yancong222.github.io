Hello!

I am a postdoctoral trainee at the Feinstein Institutes. I obtained my PhD in Linguistics from Michigan State Universiy in August 2021, with an additional specialization in Cognitive Science. I'm interested in language meaning, specifically, the enriched meaning of degree constructions in context (e.g., adjectives such as tall).

Interests

My main interests are semantics and pragmatics. This means I study how language users reason about meanings. Computational models, neurology equipment, and behavioral experiments are used in my investigation. Projects details can be found in GitHub scripts and in my CV.

Currently, I am using language models and natural language processing to better understand psychiatric disorders. We seek to understand the brain changes through the lens of language, so that preventions can be developed. Projects details can be found in GitHub scriptscz.
