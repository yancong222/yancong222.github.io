Yan Cong

I am a 5th year graduate student in the Linguistics program at Michigan State University, with an additional specialization in Cognitive Science. I am interested in language meaning and language in context. Currently I am investigating the (enriched) meaning of degree constructions in context.

Experience

  • In the Timing, Attention, and Perception (TAP) Lab [08/2019 - ] I am interested in the roles of rhythm and pitch in language processing.
  • In the MSU Neurolinguistics Lab [01/2017- ] I have done research on prosodic lengthening and semantics attraction.
  • At the Hong Kong Polytechnic University [03/2015 - 07/2016] I have done corpus studies on world Chineses variations.
  • At the Hong Kong Baptist University [01-03/2015] I have done research about syntax and phonology.
